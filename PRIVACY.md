# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how The Right Draft LLC collects and uses information submitted through the website contact form.

## Information collected

The site collects only the following information from prospective clients:

- Name
- Firm or practice
- Email address
- Inquiry message

## Purpose

Information submitted through the contact form is used only to respond to prospect questions and service inquiries.

## Third parties

- The form is configured to use Netlify Forms for submission handling.
- Contact information is not shared with third parties beyond the form provider.

## Email and phone details

Email and phone details are intentionally left blank on the site until they are finalized.

## Analytics

This site uses Plausible Analytics to measure site traffic and user behavior.

- Plausible does not use cookies and does not track personal data.
- No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected by Plausible.
- For Plausible's privacy policy, see: https://plausible.io/privacy
