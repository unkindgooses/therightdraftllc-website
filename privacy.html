Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

The Right Draft ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at therightdraftllc.com or contact us about our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, firm or practice name, and any details you include in your inquiry form message.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and browser type, collected automatically through Google Analytics.
  • Communications — emails or messages you send us directly.

We do not collect payment information through this website. We do not collect Social Security numbers, client case details, or any confidential legal matter information through the contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss how we can assist your firm
  • To provide document preparation services you engage us for
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

3. Confidentiality of Client Documents

Any legal documents, case information, or client data shared with us in the course of providing services are treated as strictly confidential. We do not disclose, sell, or share client materials with any third party. Documents are stored in encrypted environments and deleted upon request or project completion.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our website (such as Google Analytics and Web3Forms), under confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights or safety of The Right Draft or others

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand site traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect anonymized data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your request and maintain a business relationship. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any future marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at hector@therightdraftllc.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Document transfers are conducted through encrypted channels upon request.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites such as Calendly. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy
