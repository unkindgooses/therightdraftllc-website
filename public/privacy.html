Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  |  Last updated: June 13, 2026

The Right Draft is an independent freelance legal document preparation service. We are not a law firm, not attorneys, and do not provide legal advice in any form. This policy governs information collected through our website and in the course of providing document preparation services only.

1. Who We Are

The Right Draft is a freelance legal document preparation business owned and operated by Hector A. Chavez, operating in the State of Florida. We prepare, format, and organize legal documents for probate and estate planning matters strictly at the direction of licensed attorneys and law firms. We are not licensed to practice law and do not provide legal counsel, legal opinions, or legal representation of any kind.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, firm or practice name, and any details you include in your inquiry form or communications.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type, collected automatically through Google Analytics (anonymized).
  • Communications — emails or messages sent to us directly regarding our services.

We do not collect payment information through this website. We do not solicit, collect, or store Social Security numbers, case numbers, personal client identifying information, or any sensitive legal matter details through our contact form. Any such information shared with us in the course of engaged document preparation work is governed by a separate confidentiality understanding established at engagement.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss potential document preparation services
  • To perform document preparation tasks you have engaged us for under the supervision of your licensed attorney
  • To improve our website experience
  • To comply with applicable law

We do not use your information for any marketing to third parties, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information under any circumstances.

4. Confidentiality of Shared Materials

All documents, case information, and client related materials shared with us in connection with engaged document preparation services are treated as strictly confidential. The Right Draft will not disclose, distribute, reproduce, or use such materials for any purpose other than the specific document preparation task for which they were shared. Materials are stored in encrypted environments and purged upon project completion or upon your request.

You acknowledge that by sharing case materials with us for document preparation purposes, you are doing so under the supervision and authorization of a licensed attorney responsible for that matter, and that The Right Draft bears no independent legal obligation to the underlying clients of your firm.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with any third parties except in the following narrow circumstances:

  • With website service providers (Google Analytics, Web3Forms) who operate under their own privacy policies and assist in website functionality only
  • When required by a valid court order, subpoena, or applicable law
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of The Right Draft

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. Cookies may be used to track session behavior. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Blocking cookies does not affect your ability to use or contact us through this website.

7. Data Retention

Contact inquiry information is retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain a professional relationship. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us directly, and we will honor that request within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at hector@therightdraftllc.com. We do not discriminate against anyone for exercising their privacy rights.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Document delivery upon request is conducted through encrypted channels. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party platforms such as Calendly. The Right Draft is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit through links on our website.

11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for legal professionals and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our website or services following any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
